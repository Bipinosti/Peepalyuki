About Us

Welcome to Peepal Yuki, where the flavors of Nepal meet the soul of Japan in a delightful fast-food experience. Born from a passion for traditional taste and modern convenience, we bring together the best of both worlds—authentic Nepali spices and hearty Japanese comfort.

Our name reflects our roots:

    "Peepal" symbolizes the sacred tree of wisdom and life in Nepali culture.
      "Yuki" (雪) means “snow” in Japanese, representing purity, freshness, and harmony.

        At Peepal Yuki, we believe food is more than just a meal—it's a story. Our carefully crafted fusion menu celebrates street food culture, comfort bowls, and home-style recipes, all prepared with fresh ingredients, warm hospitality, and a dash of creativity.

        We’re proud to be a neighborhood favorite for both locals and visitors seeking something unique yet familiar. Whether you're grabbing a quick Momo Don, craving butter chicken ramen, or exploring our seasonal fusion specials, every dish at Peepal Yuki tells a tale of tradition, culture, and community.

        Join us and experience the flavor of Traditional & Cultural Fusion—fast, fresh, and unforgettable.
